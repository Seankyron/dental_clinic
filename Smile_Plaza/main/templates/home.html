Treatments

   Dental Consultation

Meet with the dentist and have your teeth and mouth checked to discuss your oral health concerns and needs.

     Orthodontics

Say goodbye to your misaligned teeth and jaw issues with braces (orthodontic treatment) and achieve a beautifully aligned and healthy smile.

    Oral Prophylaxis

Dental cleaning paves the way for a lifetime of confident smiles, by preventing cavities, gum diseases, and bad breath.

    Tooth Restoration

Don't let dental issues hold you back. A variety of tooth restoration treatments will help you revive your broken teeth.

    Tooth Extraction

In certain situations, extraction becomes the solution to prevent complications or alleviate pain and discomfort.

     Odontectomy

Wisdom teeth may not always be wise hence, surgical odontectomy can be essential. This specialized procedure targets impacted or troublesome wisdom teeth, ensuring a pain-free smile experience.

      Dentures

Choose dentures to complete your natural smile. Go for a full smile restoration or a partial dentures to fill in the gap.

  Removable Partial Dentures

Elevate your smile with our Removable Partial Dentures – from Standard Stayplate to Metal Framework and Flexible options. Choose confidence, choose comfort!

   Fixed Partial Dentures

Explore the seamless beauty of Jacket Crowns and Bridges for a radiant and enduring dental solution. Elevate your confidence, embrace perfection!
